ABOUT THE MOVIE

Set in the mesmerising forests of Arunachal, Bhediya tells the story of Bhaskar, a man who gets bitten by a mythical wolf, and begins
to transform into the creature himself. As Bhaskar and his ragtag buddies try to find answers, a bunch of twists, turns and laughs ensue.
